Past, Present, Future

I've always been a very creative person. Ever since I can remember I've been building Lego, drawing, or crafting one thing or another.

This then lead to me being very interested in graphic design. When I was 12 years old, I designed my first logo. Ever since then, I've spent a lot of my time doing design work.

All the time I have left, I try to spend working on my passions.

PLANS AND DREAMS

I have big plans and even bigger dreams. Eventually I want to make a name for myself, not only in the design industry but also in the various other fields I intend to branch out to.

You might say I have quite a lot on my plate. It's true, that's why I've already started.
